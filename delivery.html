Delivery, payment

Free delivery of orders over 5,000 $

In our store, all orders over $ 5,000 are delivered free of charge throughout the uk. Free shipping applies to all shipping methods and is immediately visible when placing an order. However, there are several limitations.

  • Delivery by CDEK and transport companies is carried out to the terminal or point of delivery in your city
  • Promotional goods are delivered free of charge only within London, delivery to other regions at regular rates
  • Does not work on boats from the WinBoat company and all sledges, due to their dimensions, transportation is calculated individually
  • Delivery of Suzuki, Tohatsu and Mercury motors are only delivered within UK
  • Rigid packaging, if necessary, is carried out at the expense of the buyer, cargo insurance for free shipping is paid by us
  • For distant regions of the UK, delivery is calculated individually

Payment for goods

If you are in London, then you can pay in cash to the courier upon receipt of the goods, or you can come to our store and pay there. Another payment method is bank transfer. When placing an order on the website, the system will offer you to print a receipt, where all the necessary fields will already be filled in - you just need to make a payment on this receipt at any bank. Within 3 days, the money will be credited to our account, we take the paid goods to the transport company and send you a confirmation of payment and the invoice number, by which you can track the progress of the parcel on the website of the transport company.

Payment by credit card

Payment is made through the payment gateway of PJSC UK BANK. When paying for an order with a bank card, payment processing (including entering the card number) takes place on a secure page of the processing system that has passed international certification. This means that your confidential data (card details, registration data, etc.) do not enter the online store, their processing is completely protected and no one, including our online store, can receive the client's personal and banking data. When working with card data, the information security standard developed by the international payment systems Visa and MasterCard - Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) is used, which ensures the secure processing of the details of the Holder's Bank Card. The applied data transmission technology guarantees security for transactions with Bank cards by using the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL), Verified by Visa, Secure Code protocols, and closed banking networks with the highest degree of protection. In case of a refund, the funds are returned to the same card with which the payment was made.
